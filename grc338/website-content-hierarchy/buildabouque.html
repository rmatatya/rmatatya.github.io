Build a Bouquet

Steps to build a bouquet

  1. Pick what type of bouquet you want
  2. Browse the flowers and greenery options
  3. Begin pulling out your desired flowers and greenery
  4. Arrange your bouquet
  5. Ask for assistance from our florists
  6. Finish your bouquet by wrapping in with a bow and newspaper
  7. Checkout with one of our florists
  8. Enjoy a beautiful bouquet
