Introduction

The Flower Bar

The Flower Bar is a floral shop that is set up so that people can come in and make their own bouquets. The flower and greenery will be set up along a bar with prices indicated for each flower. This way everyone can create a unique floral bouque!

Target audience

The people that use this site are those who are interested in going to The Flower Bar and want to find out information about how it works. These people include anyone who enjoys flowers. This could include a significant other wanting to surprise their loved one, a father surprising his daughter, or any perosn who simply wants to treat themselves to a beautiful and unique bouque.

Their primary tasks and goals when using this website is to learn the prices, hours, and location of The Flower Bar.

About Us

The Flower Bar is based out of Northern California in San Francisco and is owned by Rachel Matatyaou. Rachel wanted to share her love for flowers by making the process of buying flowers more fun and interactive! She loved watching her grandmother teach floral arrangement classes as a child, and as she grew up Rachel made buying flowers and designing a bouquet and weekly habit. This simple activity always brightens her day and she cannot wait to share her love for flowers with others!

Build a Bouquet

Steps to build a bouquet

  1. Pick what type of bouquet you want
  2. Browse the flowers and greenery options
  3. Begin pulling out your desired flowers and greenery
  4. Arrange your bouquet
  5. Ask for assistance from our florists
  6. Finish your bouquet by wrapping in with a bow and newspaper
  7. Checkout with one of our florists
  8. Enjoy a beautiful bouquet
