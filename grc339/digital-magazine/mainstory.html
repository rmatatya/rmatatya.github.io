Spotlight: Roman & Williams

The partnership of Stephen Alesch and Robin Standefer is at the core of Roman and Williams. This is a love story. Born in New York, Standefer studied painting in college and at New York’s Art Students League before working for gallerist Leo Castelli. Alesch, meanwhile, was raised in California and completed a traditional ten-year apprenticeship in the offices of several L.A. architects. Alesch and Standefer met in 1992 on a film set. Over the course of a decade, their professional collaboration became a personal relationship. Roman and Williams springs from their complementary strengths. To this day, Alesch renders all of Roman and Williams’ presentation drawings by hand, providing a visual narrative that expresses the DNA of every project. Standefer then translates that narrative, articulating the vision to make it happen. Standefer and Alesch have received hundreds of awards for their work, including the Cooper Hewitt National Design Award for Excellence in Interior Design, the Sir John Soane’s Museum Foundation Visionary Award, and the Palladio Award. They split their time between New York, where their practice is headquartered, and Montauk, which serves as a studio and laboratory for their designs and ideas.

Their work is characterized by a deep understanding of history and culture, as well as a commitment to craftsmanship and authenticity. Each project is a meticulous exploration of space, light, and materiality, resulting in environments that are both functional and poetic. From luxury hotels to iconic residences, Roman and Williams' portfolio is a testament to their ability to create immersive experiences that resonate with clients and audiences alike. As they continue to push boundaries and challenge conventions, Alesch and Standefer remain at the forefront of the design world, inspiring generations of designers to come.

Roman and Williams' approach to interior design is deeply rooted in storytelling and emotion. They believe that a well-designed space should evoke a sense of narrative and create an emotional connection with its inhabitants. This philosophy is reflected in their meticulous attention to detail, where every element, from furniture to lighting, is carefully chosen to contribute to the overall narrative of the space.

One of the hallmarks of Roman and Williams' interior design is their ability to seamlessly blend different styles and eras. They are known for their eclectic yet harmonious aesthetic, which combines vintage pieces with modern elements to create a timeless and elegant look. This approach not only adds layers of interest to a space but also allows for a more personalized and unique design that speaks to the client's individuality.

Their approach to interior design prioritizes sustainability and craftsmanship. They value materials and artisanal work, often sourcing from local craftsmen and employing sustainable practices. This dedication to quality and environmental responsibility ensures the durability of their designs while promoting a conscious and eco-friendly approach to interior design.
