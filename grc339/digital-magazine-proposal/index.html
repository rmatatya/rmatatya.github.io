Introduction

Architectural Digest

The overarching focus of my magazine, titled Grounded, is interior design. I plan on using the content from my GrC 440 Magazine for this project. The contents of my magazine focus on luxury residential interior design. It looks closely at elevating a home, whether you are working with a designer or not. Some articles I plan on including include the following: Editor’s Letter, Gallery of Works, Quiet Luxury, The Art of Mixing, Spotlight: Roman & Williams, Eco-Friendly Interiors, Refined Living, Small Space Living, It’s All About the Details, Bold Colors and Prints, and Design Your Dream Home.
